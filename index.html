About


Tennis has been my hobby for a bit more than a decade now. I started playing tennis since I was about 6 under the tutelage of my grandad.

I like to play all racket sports but I love tennis because the slower pace of the game compared to badminton/table tennis allows me to enjoy the game better. The feeling of striking the ball nicely with a tennis racket is also unbeatable. Last of all, tennis is a gentleman's game ;)
