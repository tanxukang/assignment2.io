TRAINING

Hall Tennis


After a two-year hiatus during NS, I decided to pick up the sport again by joining the Pioneer Hall team in NTU. The people there are really friendly and I enjoy myself a lot every session. It reignited my passion for the game, stronger than ever!

We train every week and it has been really nice to hit with different people as I get to practice striking different types of balls. We often end of the session by playing some doubles games, which can be quite fun :)
